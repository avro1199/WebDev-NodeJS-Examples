About School

S.B. Govt. Girls' Highool, situated on the Snadha,is an advanced institution for female education. The school was established by Satyen Banerjee and some local educationists in 1934 and had become nationalized in 1986. The school was acknowledged the best school in Pirojpur district by BSB foundation in 2010. Taking part in different public examinations, the students of this school have passed with glorious result.Their performance in literature and culture is outstanding. The students play an extra-ordinaryrole in observing the National Days. They also pay voluntary services during natural calamities.
